# Getting Started with Dashpad

Dashpad is a free, browser-based workspace that lets you arrange 50+ tools on a single infinite canvas — no installs, no sign-up required.

## Basic Controls

- **Add a tool** — Click the **+** button in the top center to browse all available tools.
- **Move cards** — Grab the title bar of any card and drag it anywhere on the canvas.
- **Resize cards** — Drag the handle in the bottom-right corner of any card.
- **Remove a card** — Click the **×** in the card header, or use the gear menu.

## Organizing Your Workspace

Dashpad uses a **grid canvas** — everything snaps to a 32px grid for clean alignment.

> **Tip:** Use Ctrl+Scroll (or Cmd+Scroll on Mac) to zoom in and out. Zoom centers on your cursor position.

## Saving Your Layout

Your layout is **automatically saved to your browser** as you work.

To sync across devices, **sign in** with the button in the top-right corner. Once signed in, save named layouts and switch between them from the layout switcher in the header.

## Customizing Tool Cards

1. Click the **gear icon** in a card's header.
2. Change the **emoji icon** or **label**.
3. Click **Save** — stored per card.

## Keyboard Shortcuts

| Action | Shortcut |
|---|---|
| Zoom in | Ctrl / Cmd + Scroll up |
| Zoom out | Ctrl / Cmd + Scroll down |
| Add tool | Click **+** in header |

---

Use the **?** button on any tool card to open its guide, or browse all guides from the panel header.
